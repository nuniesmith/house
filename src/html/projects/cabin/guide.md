## 1. Foundation and Structural Systems

### 1.1 Foundation System

**Foundation Type**: Crawl space with poured concrete walls
- **Depth**: 4 feet below grade
- **Wall thickness**: 8 inches reinforced concrete
- **Footings**: 20" × 8" continuous reinforced concrete
- **Vapor barrier**: 6-mil polyethylene over gravel
- **Insulation**: R-15 rigid foam on interior walls
- **Ventilation**: Cross-ventilation with foundation vents

**Site Preparation**:
- Excavation to 4' depth with proper slopes
- Gravel base: 6" compacted #57 stone
- Perimeter drainage with filter fabric
- Foundation survey and layout

### 1.2 Floor Framing System

**Floor System**:
- **Sill plates**: Pressure-treated 2×8 with anchor bolts
- **Floor joists**: 2×12 @ 16" O.C. spanning up to 22'
- **Beam support**: 4×12 engineered beam at center
- **Subfloor**: 3/4" tongue-and-groove plywood
- **Insulation**: R-30 fiberglass batts between joists

**Special Considerations**:
- Doubled joists under load-bearing walls
- Solid blocking at mid-span for stability
- Proper joist hangers and connections
- Moisture barrier under subfloor

### 1.3 Wall Framing System

**Exterior Walls**:
- **Studs**: 2×6 @ 16" O.C. for insulation depth
- **Plates**: Double top plate, single bottom plate
- **Headers**: Engineered lumber for spans over 4'
- **Sheathing**: 1/2" OSB with house wrap
- **Corners**: Three-stud corners for insulation

**Interior Walls**:
- **Studs**: 2×4 @ 16" O.C. for non-load bearing
- **Load bearing**: 2×6 construction where required
- **Sound insulation**: R-13 batts in bathroom walls

### 1.4 Roof System

**Roof Structure**:
- **Rafters**: 2×12 @ 24" O.C. for 10:12 pitch
- **Ridge beam**: 4×12 engineered lumber, 38' span
- **Roof struts**: 2×6 and 2×8 for stability
- **Sheathing**: 3/4" plywood for metal roofing
- **Insulation**: R-38 blown-in cellulose

**Roof Features**:
- Exposed rafter tails for rustic appearance
- Generous overhangs for weather protection
- Cathedral ceiling in great room
- Structural ridge beam eliminates need for interior bearing

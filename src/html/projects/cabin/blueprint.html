CONSTRUCTION SPECIFICATIONS

FOUNDATION & STRUCTURE

  • Poured concrete foundation: 8" thick walls
  • Footer: 20" × 8" reinforced concrete
  • Floor joists: 2×12 @ 16" O.C.
  • Wall framing: 2×6 @ 16" O.C.
  • Roof rafters: 2×12 @ 24" O.C.
  • Ridge beam: 4×12 engineered lumber

EXTERIOR FINISHES

  • Siding: Cedar clapboard
  • Roof: Standing seam metal
  • Foundation: Natural stone veneer
  • Windows: Double-hung, wood frame
  • Doors: Solid wood with glass panels
  • Chimney: Natural stone construction

INTERIOR SYSTEMS

  • Insulation: R-21 walls, R-38 attic
  • Flooring: Hardwood throughout
  • Heating: Wood stove + mini-splits
  • Plumbing: Copper and PEX distribution
  • Electrical: 200-amp service
  • Interior: Drywall with wood trim
